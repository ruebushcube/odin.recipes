Old Fashioned

Description:

The Old-Fashioned is a classic cocktail that has been around for over a century now. It's ability to utilize sweet and bitter ingredients without masking the liqor make it the perfect cocktail for whiskey lovers.

It's also a simple recipe that can be broken down to 3 ingredients if needed, making it easy for beginners to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. whiskey of choice
  • 0.4 oz. simple syrup (or sugar cube)
  • 3 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 2 orange peels (optional)
  • Lighter/Match (optional)
  • 1 Luxardo cherry (optional)
  • Ice
  • 1 Rocks glass

Steps

  1. Add whiskey, simple syrup, 1 orange peel, and bitters to a stirring glass
  2. Muddle 1 orange peel
  3. Add ice and stir
  4. Strain into rocks glass
  5. Add large ice cube and Luxardo cherry
  6. Flash 1 orange peel and drop into glass
  7. Enjoy!
