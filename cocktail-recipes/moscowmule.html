Moscow Mule

Description:

The Moscows Mule is a fanstic, refreshing drink with notes of citrus and ginger. Served in an ice cold copper cup, it's sure to take the edge off on a hot summer day!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can/bottle ginger beer
  • 2 oz. vodka (Feel free to change up with your choice of spirit!)
  • 1 oz. fresh squeezed lime
  • Splash of ginger simple syrup (optional)
  • Splash of honey (optional)
  • Sprig of mint (optional)
  • Copper mug
  • Ice

Steps:

  1. Combine vodka, lime, ginger beer, ginger simple, and honey into copper mug
  2. Fill mug with ice
  3. Fill to top with ginger beer
  4. Garnish with sprig of mint
  5. Enjoy!
