Whiskey Sour

Description:

The Whiskey Sour is an excellent citrus cocktail that is sweet but also tangy. Both elegant in presentation and pleasant to drink, this cocktail is ideal for classy occasions!

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. whiskey of choice
  • 1 oz. simple syrup
  • 1 oz. fresh squeezed lemon
  • 1 egg white (optional)
  • Angostura Bitters (optional)
  • Ice
  • Coupe glass

Steps

  1. Measure and pour whiskey, simple syrup, lemon juice, and egg white into shaker
  2. If using egg white, dry shake for 20-30 seconds. Then, add ice and shake for 5-10 more seconds
  3. Double strain into coupe glass
  4. If using egg white, lightly drop bitters onto surface. Feel free to draw a design using a toothpick!
  5. Enjoy!
